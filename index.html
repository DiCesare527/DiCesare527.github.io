Population:

Tampa's population is listed at nearly 400,000 individuals! With 399,734 residents, it ranks along the 3rd most populous city in Florida, and the 51st most populous
in all of the United States. Between the years of 2010 and 2020, Tampa Fl experienced a growth of roughly 27%, nearly tripling their population. Tampa is
a fast-growing city, who's projections seems to be on the rise and not slowing down anytime soon.
